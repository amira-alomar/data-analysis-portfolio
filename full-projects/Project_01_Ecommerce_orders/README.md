E-Commerce Orders & Revenue Analysis

Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to evaluate business performance across orders, revenue, product categories, fulfillment methods, shipping service levels, sales channels, and geographic regions.
The goal is to identify operational issues, revenue leakage, and concentration risks, and to provide data-driven recommendations.


---

Key Findings
