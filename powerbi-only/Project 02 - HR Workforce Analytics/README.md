HR Workforce Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

Project Overview

This is an interactive HR Workforce Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to provide a comprehensive view of employee distribution, turnover, absenteeism, and tenure.
The dashboard is designed to support HR decision-making by presenting clear KPIs and actionable insights.


---

Dashboard Pages

1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides key HR metrics and workforce distribution:

KPI Cards: Total Employees, Employees Left, Turnover Rate, Average Tenure

Charts:

Turnover Rate by Department

Overtime vs Attrition

Age Group Distribution (optional)


Slicers: Department, Gender, Job Role, Marital Status


> Purpose: Quick, executive-level insight into workforce composition and trends.




---

2️⃣ Attrition & Turnover Analysis

Detailed analysis of employee attrition by department, role, age group, and overtime:

Charts:

Employees Left by Department

Turnover Rate by Job Role

Employees Left by Age Group

Overtime vs Attrition

Salary vs Tenure by Attrition (Scatter Plot)



> Purpose: Identify high-risk areas and patterns to inform HR interventions.




---

3️⃣ Absenteeism & Tenure Insights

Analyzes tenure, promotions, work-life balance, training, and performance:

Charts:

Tenure Distribution

Promotion Gap

Work-Life Balance vs Attrition

Training vs Performance



> Purpose: Assess employee stability, training impact, and factors influencing attrition.




---

Measures (DAX Calculations)

Total Employees: Counts all employees

Employees Left: Counts employees who have left (Attrition = Yes)

Turnover Rate: Employees Left ÷ Total Employees

Average Tenure: Average YearsAtCompany

Overtime Rate: Percentage of employees working overtime


> These measures are dynamic and respond to filters across the dashboard.
